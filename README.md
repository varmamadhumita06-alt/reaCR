🌸 EmpowerHer Kerala

EmpowerHer Kerala is a women-centric web platform built to support and promote skill development among women in Kerala.
This application connects skilled women (listers) with potential clients, creating economic opportunities and fostering entrepreneurship.

💡 Problem Statement

Many talented women possess skills like:

Mehendi

Crochet

Handicrafts

Art

Cooking

Home-based services

However, they often lack:

Visibility

Market access

Structured digital platforms

Safe community-based marketplaces

EmpowerHer Kerala bridges this gap.

🌷 Solution

A women-exclusive platform where:

👩‍🎨 Women can list their skills and services

🛍 Clients can discover local women-led businesses

💬 Clients can send structured message requests

🛡 Safe and controlled communication system

The platform focuses exclusively on women residing in Kerala.
